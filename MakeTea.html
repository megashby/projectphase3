Learn to make your own boba tea!

Here is a quick link on how to make boba tea at home.

In order to make your own Boba Tea, you need to get Boba Tapioca Pearls. These can be found at Asian grocery stores or online. After making Boba Tea, in order to make the pearls stay soft, keep them refridgerated with a simple syrup, and boil for 5-10 minutes when you desire to eat them. You can also add powders and sweetened condensed milk to add sweetness to your Tapioca Pearls.

Simple Frozen Milk Wintermelon Boba Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 16 oz ice
  • 16 oz milk
  • Cooked Tapioca Pearls
  • 2 oz Wintermelon Syrup

Directions

  • Blend ice and milk until smooth
  • Add cooked Tapioca Pearls and Wintermelon Syrup in a glass
  • Add blended ice and milk
  • Stir and enjoy!
